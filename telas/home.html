The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Education

Abstract:

This paper explores the transformative impact of artificial intelligence (AI) on the education sector. It examines how AI technologies, such as machine learning and natural language processing, are being integrated into various aspects of education, from personalized learning experiences to automated administrative tasks...
